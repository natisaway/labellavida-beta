Care With Dignity & Compassion

Supporting health, independence, and quality of life in a safe and welcoming environment.

Contact Us Learn More

We are a state-approved, community-based adult day care program that provides health, rehabilitation, and recreational services to individuals who need assistance to live independently.

This allows individuals to continue living at home and avoid placement in a nursing facility, while still receiving quality care in a safe and supportive environment.

Our priority is to maintain the quality of life within our families and communities, while giving caregivers peace of mind.

Here, we want you to feel at home — as part of the La Bella Vida Family.

Who Benefits?

Adults limited by injury, disease, or frailty of age
Individuals with memory loss or dementia
Patients with Alzheimer’s or Parkinson’s
Those needing help with daily living activities
Stroke, heart attack, or accident recovery
Homebound adults needing social interaction
Adults experiencing depression or psychiatric conditions

Medical Benefits

  • Medical supervision by nurses & trained staff
  • Individual care assessments
  • Medication management & injections
  • Wound care, tube feeding, tracheal care
  • Blood sugar & oxygen monitoring
  • Mobility & daily living assistance
  • PT, OT & Speech Therapy coordination
  • Ongoing case management

Recreational Services

We focus on the client’s strengths, as well as weaknesses, and use every interaction as an opportunity to help them feel good about themselves and their time spent with us.

We have several special interest clubs where our participants can meet and share their life experiences. Special activities such as exercise, computer and English classes, and weekly field trips to museums, parks, and other cultural centers enhance the La Bella Vita experience.

Activities Included Weekly

  • Arts & crafting
  • Music and sing-a-longs
  • Reading and discussion groups
  • Card and table games
  • Puzzles
  • Active games
  • Cooking
  • Storytelling
  • Bingo
  • Computers for memory enhancement programs
  • Musical guests
  • Volunteer interactions
  • Birthdays and special parties
  • Devotions and reminiscing

Nutrition

Our licensed dietitian creates a daily menu that provides our participants with balanced nutrition, and tasty, well presented meals that assure a daily intake of needed nutrients.

*Special diets are easily accommodated and assistance at meal time is always available.

Beauty Salon

Your loved one can enjoy having their hair cut and styled, as well as receive a relaxing manicure.

For participants, regular beauty appointments can be scheduled with the beauty salon through the center. It gives your loved ones just one more special reason to attend.

Services Include

  • Hair cuts & styling
  • Manicures
  • Scheduled appointments
  • On-site convenience

Transportation

In addition, we are happy to arrange senior transportation to and from all medical appointments outside of our center.

If necessary, we will also help you with the required paperwork.

Transportation Support

  • Medical appointments
  • Door-to-door coordination
  • Paperwork assistance
  • Caregiver peace of mind

Volunteers

Helping caregivers and adults whose activities are limited by injury, disease, or the frailties of age can be personally invigorating and deeply rewarding.
